What Is The Utopia Project?

Humanity now has the technological ability to provide food, housing, healthcare, and meaningful lives for everyone. Yet many of our systems were designed centuries ago.

The Utopia Project is an open exploration of what a society built for human flourishing could look like.

Questions We Explore

  • How should work function in an automated world?
  • How can technology benefit everyone?
  • What economic systems support well-being?
  • How can communities thrive?
  • What does true sustainability look like?

Get Involved
